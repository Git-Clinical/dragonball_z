Omniversal Genki Dama? (Spirit Bomb)

OGxBeerus 08/30/16
11
10

We all know that the Genki Dama is powered from the life it absorbs from the plants, animals, and people around it. Goku has even gotten energy from far away planets using this technique.

What if in the future of Dragon Ball Super Goku, has to draw energy from all of the surrounding Universes!? Is this even possible? How powerful would this Spirit Bomb be? Would it be enough to destroy the Omni-King himself? Interesting theory I conjured up so I thought I'd share it! Let me know what you guys think!
